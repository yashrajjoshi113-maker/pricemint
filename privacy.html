Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What we collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password (handled entirely by our authentication provider, Supabase — we never see or store your plaintext password). If you set a price alert, we store the product and your target price against your account. We don't collect payment information — all purchases happen on the retailer's own site.

2. Local device storage

Your wishlist (heart icon) and recently-viewed products are stored only in your browser's local storage, not on our servers, so they stay on the device you used to set them.

3. How we use your data

We use your account data solely to run the price-alert feature and to sign you in. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Affiliate links

"Go to Store" and "Visit" buttons take you to the retailer's site through an affiliate link. This may earn PriceMint a small commission if you make a purchase — it never changes the price you pay. See our Affiliate Disclosure for details.

5. Cookies

We use a small number of cookies/local storage entries to remember your login session and cookie preference. We don't use third-party advertising trackers.

6. Your choices

You can delete your price alerts at any time from the Price Alerts page, or request full account deletion by contacting us.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out at support@pricemint.example.
